Alibag Villa Interior: What to Expect from Start to Finish

A step-by-step breakdown of how HSI OS manages the lifecycle of a premium luxury execution.

A common friction point between homeowners and interior execution teams is the ambiguity of the timeline. "When will it be ready?" is a question that standard contractors answer with guesswork. At HSI, we answer it with data.

Here is exactly how a premium Alibag villa interior transitions from a raw shell to a handed-over home, managed entirely through HSI OS.

Phase 1: Project Intake & Digital Onboarding (Weeks 1-2)

Before a single hammer is swung, the project must exist completely in the digital realm.

  • We intake the architect's Good For Construction (GFC) drawings.
  • The Project Planning Engine translates these drawings into hundreds of trackable micro-tasks.
  • Materials are quantified (MTOs generated) and the initial master budget is locked on your dashboard.

Phase 2: Core and Shell Rectification (Weeks 3-5)

Most bare-shell structures require preparatory work before premium finishes can be applied.

  • Waterproofing the terraces, wet areas, and ground-floor walls.
  • Core cutting for HVAC ducts and laying the foundational conduits for home automation.
  • Plaster leveling—because premium marble cannot be laid on an uneven subfloor.

Homeowner View: Through HSI OS, you receive photos of raw plumbing and electrical lines being pressure-tested.

Phase 3: Initial Millwork & Ceiling (Weeks 6-11)

This is where the skeleton of the interior is built.

  • Gypsum false ceiling framing and boarding.
  • Installation of internal door frames and the primary BWP Marine Ply carcasses for wardrobes and kitchens.
  • First coat of wall primer and putty to seal the environment.

Phase 4: Finishes & High-End Assembly (Weeks 12-16)

The space finally begins to look like a home.

  • Italian marble or premium stone is laid, cured, and mirror-polished.
  • Veneers are applied to the casework, and PU/melamine spraying begins in dust-free chambers.
  • Air conditioning final cassettes and automation panels are installed.

Homeowner View: If a specific imported stone jumps in price, the Cost Intelligence module sends a change approval request to your phone before procurement.

Phase 5: Styling, Snagging, and Handover (Weeks 17-18)

The final 5% of execution takes 20% of the effort.

  • Deep cleaning of the villa.
  • Our internal engineers conduct a 200-point digital snagging process via the HSI OS app.
  • Loose furniture arrives, curtains are hung, and final styling is complete.
