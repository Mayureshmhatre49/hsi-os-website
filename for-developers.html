FAQ

Questions from Developers

Can HSI OS scale across multiple projects running simultaneously?

+

Yes. HSI OS is built specifically for multi-project developers. You get a portfolio-level dashboard showing status, cost, and milestones across all your active projects simultaneously. Vendor relationships, procurement data, and execution playbooks are shared across projects — reducing overhead and improving consistency.

Do my internal teams need training to use HSI OS?

+

HSI provides full onboarding, training, and go-live support as part of the deployment. Our team works alongside your execution team during initial projects to ensure smooth adoption. The platform is designed to be intuitive for project managers and site coordinators familiar with interior execution workflows.

Can HSI work with our existing architects and vendors?

+

Yes. HSI OS is agnostic to your existing vendor relationships. Your vendors, architects, and designers plug into the platform — using it for reporting, coordination, and procurement workflows. You don't need to change your partners; you need to give them a better system to work within.

What engagement model options are available for developers?

+

Developers can choose: (1) Full HSI service — where our team manages execution for your projects using HSI OS, (2) Platform-only — where your internal team uses HSI OS independently with our support, or (3) Hybrid — where we deploy alongside your team and architects. We assess your operations and recommend the right model. Book a discovery call →
