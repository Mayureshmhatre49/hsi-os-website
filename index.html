FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HSI and HSI OS before starting your project.

What is HSI OS and how is it different from regular project management tools?

+

HSI OS is purpose-built for premium residential interior execution — not a generic tool adapted for interiors. It speaks the language of villa projects: scope definitions, trade sequencing, material lead times, vendor coordination, and client reporting. Unlike general tools like Asana or Monday, HSI OS handles real-time cost tracking, procurement workflows, and execution milestones specific to high-end interior projects in Alibag and Mumbai.

Can I use HSI OS with my existing architect or interior designer?

+

Absolutely. HSI is collaborative, not restrictive. You can retain your existing architect or interior designer and use HSI OS to manage the execution layer — tracking progress, monitoring costs, and maintaining transparency. The platform is designed to work alongside any design team, ensuring design intent is preserved while execution is managed with full accountability.

Does HSI handle villa and luxury home interiors in Alibag and Mumbai?

+

Yes. Alibag and Mumbai are HSI's two primary markets — and they share a lot in common. Both are coastal, both attract premium second-home buyers, and both demand execution quality that matches the investment. From Alibag villa projects (Casa Frangipani, Serenity Villa, Villa Vayu, Villa Bellissimo and more) to high-end Mumbai apartments and penthouses, we understand the specific execution challenges each market brings: material logistics, coastal waterproofing, local vendor ecosystems, and on-time delivery for discerning homeowners.

What is the difference between HSI as a service vs. HSI OS as a platform?

+

HSI offers two engagement models. As a service, HSI manages your entire interior execution — from planning and design coordination to on-site delivery — with our team running the process using HSI OS. As a platform, HSI OS can be used by your own architects, developers, or execution teams to manage projects independently with better structure, real-time data, and stakeholder transparency. You can also combine both — our team + our platform.

How does HSI OS help with cost transparency in interior projects?

+

HSI OS tracks every rupee across your project in real time. It categorizes spend by room, trade, or vendor — and alerts you the moment costs deviate from plan. You get a live budget dashboard showing approved spend, pending payments, and projected completion costs. This eliminates the most common homeowner nightmare: discovering at the end that your interior ran 30-40% over budget with no clear explanation.

Is HSI suitable for developers building multiple premium residences?

+

Yes. HSI OS is designed to scale across multiple units and projects. Developers can standardize execution across all their residences, maintain consistent quality benchmarks, and provide homebuyers with real-time project visibility as a differentiator. HSI works as a full execution partner or as a platform your internal team can use across your portfolio.

Does HSI work for luxury interior projects in Mumbai?

+

Yes. HSI serves premium homeowners and developers across both Alibag and Mumbai — two coastal markets with similar climates, project profiles, and premium standards. In Alibag, we bring deep on-ground execution experience from 7+ villa projects. In Mumbai, we serve high-end apartments, penthouses, and luxury residences with the same structured, platform-powered approach. The HSI OS platform ensures consistent execution intelligence regardless of location.
